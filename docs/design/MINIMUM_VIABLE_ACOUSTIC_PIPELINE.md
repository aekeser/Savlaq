# Savlaq Minimum Viable Acoustic Pipeline (MVAP)

## Goal

Produce a publication-ready acoustic dataset for the Journal of Phonetics study.

## Target measurement region

Target-vowel steady-state region.

## Phase 1

✓ Target-vowel duration

✓ Mean F0

✓ Peak F0

✓ Mean intensity

✓ F1 (midpoint)

✓ F2 (midpoint)

## Phase 2

- F0 trajectory
- Intensity trajectory
- F1 trajectory
- F2 trajectory

## Phase 3

- Signal stability
- Voice quality
- Advanced spectral measures

## Success criterion

Savlaq can process an entire experiment automatically and produce one analysis-ready dataset for R.
